Welcome

Welcome to my wonderful website all about Velociraptors. A Velociraptor is a type of dinosaur called a Therapoda. Velociraptors were carnivores, they were part hunter and part scavenger. They ate lizards and bugs and even ate large animals called Protoceratops.

Velociraptors were found in Mongolia in Asia, they used to live 80-84 million years ago in the Cretacious period.

Picture

Interesting Facts about the Velociraptor

  • It was nocturnal
  • It had feathers
  • It was warm blooded
  • It ran on two legs
  • It had razor sharp claws
  • It had small sharp teeth
